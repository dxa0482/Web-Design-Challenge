Summary Latitude vs. X

This project's purpose is to show if there is a relationship between latitude and four factors: max temperature, wind speed, humidity, and cloud cover. The city data was pulled from citypy and weather data was pulled from the OpenWeather API. 500 random cities were used for this dataset.
